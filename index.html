Core Features

01

Project Management

Manage everything from simple tasks to complex projects. Break down work into tasks, track progress with status labels, and monitor completion with visual progress bars. Access real-time dashboards for instant insights.

02

Team & Client Management

Store employees and clients in a centralized database. Assign team members to projects, manage contact information, and track statuses. Find anyone instantly with advanced search and filtering.

03

Live Collaboration

Connect face-to-face using integrated video conferencing. Monitor changes through the live activity feed. Conduct presentations and meetings without switching tools. Chat in real-time for efficient communication.

04

Calendar & Planning

Visualize projects, tasks, and events on an intuitive calendar. Track deadlines, manage team schedules, and ensure no critical dates are missed. Keep everyone aligned with centralized calendar management.

05

Notes & Documentation

Capture project notes, meeting records, and critical information in a centralized notebook. Document ideas, share insights with your team, and maintain a searchable knowledge base accessible to everyone.

06

Analytics & Reporting

Generate comprehensive reports to analyze performance and measure productivity. Export reports in various formats for stakeholders. Make data-driven decisions using real-time insights from all activities.

07

Administration & Control

Control your platform with a powerful admin panel. Manage user permissions, define access levels, and ensure security. Maintain compliance and manage all operations from one centralized location.
