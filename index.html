Core Features

PMI Compliant Enterprise Governance

International project management standards with complete compatibility. An infrastructure covering Integration, Scope, Time, Cost, Quality, Resource, Communication, Risk, Procurement, and Stakeholder management — all under one roof, faithful to methodology.

AI-Powered Decision Support

The platform doesn't just collect data, it analyzes and alerts you. It detects project risks in advance, flags performance anomalies, recommends resource optimization. Executives make decisions with insights, not data.

Role, Permission & Security Management

Create sophisticated access control with unit, role, and user-based permissions. Control who sees what, who can edit what — customize your entire access hierarchy to match your organizational structure.

Universal Integration Infrastructure

Connect to your existing ERP systems, Supply Management Portals, and Active Directory infrastructure via REST Web Services. MVP FACTORY doesn't invalidate your organization's existing technology investments — it strengthens them.

Enterprise Analytics & Reporting

Perform performance analysis at every level from portfolio to task. View the specific sections you need with detailed filters, export reports in different formats, base your executive presentations on data.

Digital Twin Method

Create a digital model of all your organization's processes. Whatever happens in the real world, the platform reflects it instantly. Generate scenarios, run simulations, test your decisions — before bringing them to life.

Project Management System (PMS): Manage Your Projects End-to-End

As teams grow, projects become complex. Tasks multiply, responsibilities blur, deadlines slip. Most tools merely make this chaos visible — they don't solve it.

MVP Factory's Project Management System (PMS) takes a different approach. It brings together scattered tools, disconnected communication, and data silos into a single integrated platform. Your organization's project data, teams, and workflows now live under one roof — transparent, traceable, and completely under your control.

Visual Dashboards

Kanban, List & Timeline views in one click. Drag-and-drop status updates with instant team visibility.

Team Management

Centralized database for team & clients. Smart assignments, contact insights, relationship tracking.

Calendar Planning

Visual milestone planning with resource conflict detection and capacity balancing.

Notes & Memory

Capture decisions, meeting notes & archives. Organizational knowledge always accessible.

Progress Reports

Real-time health monitoring with visual indicators and automatic stakeholder updates.

Live Collaboration

Built-in video conferencing, screen sharing, and activity feeds. No tool switching needed.
